Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

Easy Classic for a quick lunch or snack

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons favorite jelly

Directions

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread the jelly on the other slice
  3. Put the two slices of bread together to form a sandwich
